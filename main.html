About Us



Sports Viewer is the only website that let's you find a restaurant or bar that is playing the sporting event that you want to see, surrounded by the people you want to watch it with. Whether it's the big game, the fight of the century, the draft, or an obscure international sport, Sports Viewer will help you find the places showing them, so you won't have to waste your time calling tons of different places to see what they'll be showing for the evening. We also include what drink and food specials will be available at the different establishments, as well as what predominant fan base they are.
